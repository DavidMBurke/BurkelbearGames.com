About the Game

A World That Reacts to Everything

Grimorium is a dungeon-crawling, spell-crafting roguelike set in a fully simulated voxel world where nearly everything can be manipulated, destroyed, or weaponized. Built on a custom micro-voxel engine, every voxel is simulated and destructible — the environment isn't just a backdrop, it's a tool.

Master the elements and descend into ever more dangerous depths as your power evolves. Build devastating or precise spells using a flexible casting system with deep combinatorial freedom — the only limits are your creativity and your nerve.

Explore procedurally generated dungeons packed with secrets, battle deadly foes, and turn the environment itself into your greatest weapon. No two runs are the same.

Key Features

  • Custom micro-voxel engine — every voxel simulated and destructible
  • Flexible spell-crafting system with deep combinatorial freedom
  • Procedurally generated dungeons packed with secrets
  • Manipulate, destroy, or weaponize the environment itself
  • Roguelike progression — master the elements across ever-deeper depths
