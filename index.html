About Us

Burkelbear Games

Burkelbear Games is an independent game studio operating out of Cambridge, MA. We are a small team developing our own custom micro-voxel cellular automata game engine to allow for deep simulation and emergent gameplay along with our flagship game, Grimorium — a spell-crafting roguelike where every voxel is simulated and destructible.
